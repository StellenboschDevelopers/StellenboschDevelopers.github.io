Stellenbosch Developer Meetups

Growing the culture

Introduction

We hope to do a lot for the community and the developer culture in Stellenbosch.

If you are new here feel free to message us or come talk to us in person at any of our scheduled meetups.

Group Goals

We hope to grow as a group and in doing so grow the scale of future events. Our goal will be to give experienced developers a place to come and share ideas while new people come to be inspired and create interesting projects.

More than just code.

Strive to go the extra mile and accommodate many different aspects of technology, by giving the opportunity for anyone to talk about their apps and projects.
