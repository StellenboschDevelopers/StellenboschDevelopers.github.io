Stellenbosch Developer Meetup

Welcome to the rough draft.

We hope to do a lot for the community and the developer culture in Stellenbosch and in the rest of South Africa!

If you are new here feel free to leave a message or come talk to us in person at any of our Bi-Monthly Meetups!

Developer Goals

We hope to grow as a group and in doing so grow the scale of future projects. Our goal will be to give experienced developers a place to come and share ideas while new people come to learn valuable skills and create interesting projects.

More than just code.

Strive to go the extra mile and accommodate many different aspects of Tech, by giving the opportunity for anyone to talk about their new Idea/App/hobby.
